# Writers Studio: Platform Overview

Writers Studio is a web-based writing platform for any genre, designed to keep story data organized while reducing continuity mistakes.

## What It Solves

- Keeps characters, locations, timelines, and story notes in one place.
- Tracks cross-book and cross-chapter references.
- Helps writers catch consistency issues before publication.

## Core Capabilities

- Character and series management with shared world-building context.
- Timeline tracking across stories, chapters, and events.
- Writing dashboards for progress, goals, and productivity.
- Export workflows for moving data out of the platform.

## AI-Assisted Consistency Tools

- Detects likely timeline inconsistencies.
- Flags potential character-reference conflicts.
- Surfaces continuity warnings so authors can review quickly.

## Positioning

Writers Studio is a general writer-focused system, not tied to a single genre. It is intended for fiction and narrative teams who need structured tracking, continuity support, and practical AI assistance during drafting and revision.
