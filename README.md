# Writers Studio

Writers Studio is a continuity and canon companion that keeps your world bible, timeline, and character facts consistent across drafts and books.

Write where you already write (Word, Scrivener, Google Docs, Markdown). Writers Studio sits beside your draft so you can **find facts fast**, **spot contradictions early**, and **revise with confidence**.

## What it helps you do

- **Remember everything:** characters, locations, lore, rules, systems, and what was true in which draft.
- **Keep continuity intact:** avoid contradictions in timelines, descriptions, relationships, and world rules.
- **Revise faster:** turn “wait, what did I say earlier?” into a quick lookup and a clear fix.
- **Manage series cleanly:** track canon across multiple books without losing your mind in chapter 47.

## It adapts to your story system

Every story has its own weird (in a good way). Writers Studio adapts to your story system: create your own **lore entries, rules, resources, and relationships**, then link them to the scenes where they matter.

## Core experience

1. **Import your draft** (or paste chapters) from Word/Scrivener exports, Docs, or Markdown.
2. **Build your canon vault**: characters, locations, factions, rules, systems, items, and anything else your story needs.
3. **Link facts to scenes** so you always know where something was introduced or changed.
4. Run **confidence checks** (optional automation) to surface likely continuity risks.
5. **Resolve issues** and export reports/checklists for your next editing pass.

## Who it’s for

- Novelists writing single books or long-running series.
- Writers juggling complex casts, timelines, lore, or multi-book canon changes.
- Authors who want structure and confidence without being forced into a new writing app.

## Current focus

Writers Studio is becoming the best day-to-day continuity companion for fiction authors, prioritizing:

- smooth importing from common writing tools,
- flexible world bible + character tracking,
- timeline clarity across scenes and books,
- practical confidence checks with low noise,
- simple exports for editing and collaboration.
